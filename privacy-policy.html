Privacy Policy

Quick Notes Pro

Effective Date: June 2026

Overview

Quick Notes Pro is a lightweight browser extension designed to help users create and manage personal notes directly within their browser.

Information Collection

Quick Notes Pro does not collect personal information, account information, payment information, browsing history, or location data.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome Storage to save notes locally on the user's device. Notes remain under the user's control and are not transmitted to external servers.

Data Sharing

Quick Notes Pro does not sell, share, rent, or disclose user information to any third party.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use advertising networks, analytics services, tracking technologies, or remote data collection systems.

Permissions

  • storage — Used only for saving and retrieving notes locally.

Security

All information remains stored locally within the user's browser environment.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
